##  Find the space between two 2d vectors 

```math
\begin{bmatrix}
x_1\\
y_1\\
\end{bmatrix}
```

```math
\begin{bmatrix}
x_2\\
y_2\\
\end{bmatrix}
```

Eucledian distance of vector that is between two vectors is given by 

```math
\sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}
```

This is generalizable to n-dimensional space.
