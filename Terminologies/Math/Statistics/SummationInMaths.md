1. Example of summing 1 to 10
```math
\sum_{i=1}^{10} i
```
***means sum of i for i=1 to 10***



2. Another example:
Example of squaring 1 to 10 
```math
\sum_{i=1}^{10} i^2
```
***means sum of i^2 for i=1 to 10***


3. Another example:
```math
\sum_{i=1}^{n} f(i)
```
***means sum of f(i) for i=1 to n***
